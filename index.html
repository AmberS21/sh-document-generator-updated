How to Use This Tool
1
Choose your input
Paste candidate text, a full CV, or assignment details. Or enter a public URL. Or upload a file — PDF, Word, Excel, or a LinkedIn PDF export. Ezekia CRM will appear when MCP is ready.
2
Select a template
Use the search bar or scroll the dropdown. A live preview shows exactly which placeholders Claude will fill, the output format, and the regional scope.
3
Choose output format
Word, PowerPoint, Excel, or PDF. The format auto-selects based on the template but you can override it.
4
Batch processing
Enable to produce one branded document per candidate. Upload multiple CVs or paste multiple names — you receive a single ZIP.
5
Download from My History
All generated documents are private to you. Download, share, or regenerate at any time.
💡 Best results: paste the full text from a LinkedIn PDF export rather than a short summary.
